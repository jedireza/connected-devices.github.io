About

The Connected Devices team at Mozilla is an effort to apply our guiding principles to the Internet of Things.

We have a lot to learn and a lot to do. We want the community there from the start. We'll kick things off by enabling events (meetups/workshops).

Events

Let's meetup to present our work, hacks and ideas. Let's hold workshops where we can learn from and teach each one another.

There isn't a strict framework for events. In the end the format of an event is up to the chapter members.

Chapters

The first chapter is starting in San Francisco and we welcome chapters in other cities/regions.

Learn how to start a new chapter.

Host

We're a community driven effort and 100% open source. This means anyone can use our materials and/or host an event.

The only expectations for event hosts is that events be not-for-profit (free or low cost) and that governance of events should have an open membership model (so that nobody can 'control' the events).

You can even use the Mozilla Community Spaces for your event.

Contact

We communicate in the open and use GitHub a lot, especially the issues feature. For example; the connected-devices/discuss repo is our general discussion board. You can also reach us on Twitter @allthedevices.
